Reggaetton Beat Song

I enjoy regaetton music because I feel a sense of connection to my Latinx community.
Made using Reaper, Max. This song was my form of experiment making a fully flesh song that has a dynamic makes use synths, sampling sounds on my midi keyboard. This process helped me learn the differences and steps between creating a song melody and designing the sounds for the music. Note I do not have access to any physical instruments like drums and trumpets. Sampled sounds like snare and trumpet were from freesounds.org. Sounds have been modified from the original.

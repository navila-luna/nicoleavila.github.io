Live Performance of Granular Synthesis with Additive Synthesis

I used Max, an audio visual coding language to explore creating music from the ground up. I find Max to be a tool that cares less about making songs and more about exploring randomness and sound art. My curiosity about how some sounds played ‘randomly’ can still sound musically pleasant led me to explore granular synthesis and creating decision trees to decide when to play a certain synth.

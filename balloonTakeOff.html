Balloon TakeOff

Balloon TakeOff aims to provide a visual an auditory experience for practing the box-breathing and 4-7-8 breathing technique. This project was inspired from the breathing technique my 6th grade English teacher taught me while I was experiencing my first panic attack in middle school. My teacher took me outside of the class, sat me down and told me to imagine I was holding a deflated air balloon. He told me to imagine putting all things I'm worried about in the balloon. Once I'm done, he then tells me to begin inflating it by taking deep breaths. Once I'm done inflating, he tells me to lift the balloon up and imagine it heading higher off to the sky.

Balloon TakeOff takes one through how I felt and pictured the moment in which my teacher walked me through deep breathing.Balloon Takeoff can be viewed as a 3 step activity: Activity 1: Writing worries out. Activity 2: 3 repetitions of deep breaths Activity 3: Watching the release of the balloon.

Balloon TakeOff was made using three.js for animations and 3D visuals of the balloon and stars. HTML/CSS as well as Javascript were used for creating the styling and functionality of the game. Music was made using MAX, Reaper, and some sampled sounds. When I write, sing, or play music, I picture a scene like in a movie or music video that I am capturing. Each song plays a different scene in a story of serenity that I imagine in that very moment I play.

Party Parrot

Party parrot Sequencer was inspired by my love for parrots and the slack party parrot gif used constantly in my computer science classes. Party parrot Sequencer was made using tone.js to create synths and add sampled sounds. Animations and styling was made through HTML and CSS.

So what exactly is party parrot? Party parrot is an internet meme that originated from a BBC clip of zoologists documenting the last remaining Kakapos, a rare parrot located in New Zealand. Below is a short YouTube clip about the formation of the party parrot meme:
